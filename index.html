Chabagi Du'asing

Chak Ubagi Du'a

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِيمَا رَزَقْتَنَا وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

"Ya Allah, nahakna eikhoida pibiribasing asida barkat pinabiyu, Amasung eikhoibu meigi azabtagi kanbiyu".

Chak Chaba Houbagi Dua

بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

"Houjari Allah gi mingda, yamna thoujal heiba amasung minungsi chaoba mapuni".

Houbagi Du'a Kaoramlabadi

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ أَوَّلَهُ وَآخِرَهُ

"Ahouba amadi aroibada Allahgi mingga loinana chajari".

Marak Marakta Son-gadaba

اَللَّهُمَّ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ وَلَكَ الشُّكْرُ

"Ya Allah, thagatchariba matik magun khudingmk nahak khktagini, amasung thagatpagi matik chabasu nahak khaktani".

Chaba Loibada Son-gadaba Du'a

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا وَجَعَلَنَا مِنَ الْمُسْلِمِينَ

"Thagatchariba khudingmk Allah khaktagini, mahakna eikhoibu pijabire pithakpire amasung Musalman oihnbire".

Dawat Chaba Loibagi Du'a

اللَّهُمَّ اطْعِمَّ مَنْ اطْعَمَنِي وَاسْقِِِ ِ مَنْ سَقَانِيِ

"Ya Allah, Nahakna eibu pijabiriba amasung eibu pithakpiriba mahakpusu nahakna pijabiyu amasung pithakpiyu".

Chabada Hisaab Loudanaba Du'a

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا هَذَا وَرَزَقَنَاهُ مِنْ غَيْرِ حَوْلٍ مِنَّا وَلَا قُوَّةٍ

"Thagatchariba matik magun khudingmk Allahgini, mahakna eikhoibu chinjak pijabiri amasung rijq pinabiri, Eikhoigi pang-gal amadi shakti amata yaodana".

Chabagi Sunnat & Adaab

Chabagi Sunnatsing

  1. Khut animak khujeng faoba sengna hamdokpa, Atoppa fi amada tethoktaba.
  2. Dastarkhwan tharaga chaba.
  3. Khufm ahumgi manungdagi amada fammaga chaba.
  4. Chak chaba houbagi du'a sonba.
  5. Yetki khutna chaba.
  6. Khutsa ahumna chaba.
  7. Esagi maangdagi chaba.
  8. Yamna saba pot chadaba.
  9. Ngara souraga chadaba.
  10. Chanaba pot takhrabadi khunggatlaga chaba
  11. Chanaba potpu haote haidaba.
  12. Charaba matung khutsasing sengna lekthokpa.
  13. Chanaba pukhamda khut haamjindaba.
  14. Chaba loibagi du'a sonba.

Chabagi Adaabsing

  1. Nabigi tariqa ga loinana chanaba hotnagani.
  2. Chabadagi fanglakpa panggal aduna Allahgi Ibadat toubada sijinnage haayna khn-gani.
  3. Kok khumlaga chagani.
  4. Khut animak khujengfaoba sengna haamdokkani, atoppa fi amafaoda teithokloi.
  5. Dastarkhwan tharaga chagani.
  6. Khong-up / Chappal louthoklaga chagani.
  7. Chak Ubagi dua Songani

    اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِيمَا رَزَقْتَنَا وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

  8. kKhufam ahumgi manungda amada fmlaga chagani.
    1. Khong naamma feitharaga namma yungkhtlaga fmlaga chaba.
    2. Namazgi khufmda fmlaga chaba.
    3. Ninggaang fmlaga chaba.
  9. Mee ani nattraga henna tinnaraga chagani.
  10. Chaba houbagi du'a son-gani.
  11. Yetki khutna chagani, Khutsa ahumna chanaba hotnagani.
  12. Isagi maangdagi chagani, Mathel yaamlabadi houningba amadagi hekta houba yaay.
  13. Chaminnari mee aduda henna pijakhinaba hotnagani.

Thakpagi Du'asing

Thakpa Houbagi Du'a

بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

"Houjari Allah gi mingda, yamna thoujal heiba amasung minungsi chaoba mapuni".

Thakpa Loibagi Du'a

ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّٰهِ

"Thagatchari matik magun khudingmak Allah khaktagini".

Thakpada Hisaab Loudanaba Du'a

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي سَقَانَا عَذْبًا فُرَاتًا بِرَحْمَتِهِ وَلَمْ يَجْعَلْهُ مِلْحًا أُجَاجًا بِذُنُوبِنَا

"Thagatchariba matik magun khudingmak Allah khaktagini, mahakki rahmatna eikhoibu athumba amadi asengba ising pithkpire, amasung eikhoigi gunah na marm oiraga masibu akhaba amadi ayakpa oihnbide".

Thakpagi Sunnatsing

Thakpagi sunnat

  1. Yetki khutna pairaga thakpa.
  2. Famlaga thakpa.
  3. Yenglaga thakpa.
  4. Bismillah sollaga thakpa.
  5. Ningsa ahumda thakpa.
  6. Thaklaba matungda Alhamdulillah sonba.

Tumbagi Du'asing

Tumbagi Du'a

اللَّهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

"Ya Allah, nahakki mingga loinana sijari amasung hingjari".

Akiba Mang Mangbagi Du'a

سُبْحَانَ اللّٰهِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ وَلَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللّٰهُ وَاللّٰهُ أَكْبَرُ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللّٰهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ

"Allahdi amakpa khudingmktagi senggi, Amasung thagatchariba matik magun khudingmk allahkhktagini, amasung laatpada matik chaba allah khktani, amasung Allah di pumnamaktagi chaowee, ".

Akiba Mang Manglabadi Kari Tougani

  1. Oirom nakanda tin yaohndana ahumlak sittok-kani.
  2. Allah da sheitaan dagi kanbinaba haayjagani.
  3. Akiba / fattaba mang adugi matangda kanadasu haayroi.
  4. Hiplamliba nakal adudagi onthoklaga atoppa nakl do ollaga hipkani
  5. Ngamlabadi hougtlaga nafl namaz raka'at ani son-gani.

Tumba Hougatpagi Du'a

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ

"Thagatchariba matik magun khudingmk Allah khaktagina, mahakna eikhoibu siruraba matungda amuk hinghnbire, amasung eikhoi khudingmk mahakki maikeida amuk hallagadabani".

Tumbagi Sunnat & Adaab

Tumbagi Sunnatsing

  1. Esha Namaz matungda yaamna thuna tumnaba hotnaba., duniyagi wari wataida matam lenthoktaba.
  2. Fi hongdoklaga tumba
  3. Wudu yaona tumba
  4. Famung nattraga maitha adu ahumlak knthokpa
  5. Surma ahumlak haaplaga tumba
  6. Istigfar ahumlak sonba

    أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللهَ الْعَظِيمَ الَّذِي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ

  7. Fatima tashbih chatpa,
    • Subhanallah 33 rak سُبْحَانَ اللهِ
    • Alhamdulillah 33 rak ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّٰهِ
    • Allahu-Akbar 34 rak اللّٰهُ أَكْبَر
  8. Qul 4 sonba.
  9. Yetlomda cheplaga qiblahgi maikeida may ollaga tumba.
  10. Uplaga tumdaba.
  11. Tumbagi du'a sonba

    اللَّهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

Tumba Hougatpa gi Sunnat

  1. Tumba hougtpagi du'a sonba.
  2. Khut ki amonba asina mai amadi mitta tapna noiba.
  3. Miswak twba
